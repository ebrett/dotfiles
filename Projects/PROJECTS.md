## Project Lifecycle

1. **Idea** → Stays in Obsidian as a fleeting note until it needs tooling
2. **Active** → Lives in ~/Projects/, has an Obsidian anchor note, appears here as `active`
3. **Paused** → Update status here + write a one-sentence re-entry note in Obsidian anchor note
4. **Done** → Mark `done`, archive the Obsidian anchor note
5. **Archived** → Move to ~/Projects/_archive/ after 60 days inactive, or delete if abandoned

## Archive Rule

Any project untouched for 60 days gets moved to `_archive/` or its status set to `archived`.
The cost of archiving is near zero. The cost of 15 "active" projects is high.
